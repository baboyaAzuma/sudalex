About SudaLex

Sudalex Enterprises Limited is a multi-disciplinary company based in Juba, South Sudan, operating in three strategic sectors: Corrugated Box Manufacturing, Outdoor Advertising, and Real Estate Development. As a fully South Sudanese-owned enterprise, we are committed to delivering high-quality, sustainable, and innovative solutions that contribute to national development and client success.

We combine technical know-how with modern infrastructure and a dedicated team of professionals to serve a wide range of customers—businesses, NGOs, institutions, and the public sector. Our forward-thinking approach and dedication to quality make us one of the most trusted enterprises in South Sudan.
